Almost Done at OpenText

December 13th, 2017

It's been an interesting couple of months here at OpenText. It has been over four months at this point, but it honestly doesn't feel that way, for some reason.

The start of my job here definitely was not what I was hoping for: my manager being on vacation for my entire first week, and no one else who was actually in the office with me knowing what it was that I was supposed to be doing, exactly. My manager was out in the Ottawa office, which only made things more difficult. Compared to my last co-op, I can't exactly say that the onboarding was smooth!

I read almost the entire user documentation manual for the Content Management System here, and in the end, I only got a couple hours of experience working in the actual system. I just still wish that I did have the chance to do more writing, as well, since I do have the experience, and that is what I was hoping to get out of this work term.
